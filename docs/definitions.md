# Definitions — Myelin‑Centric Model of Strength

## White‑Matter Strength
Strength expressed through the speed, fidelity, and synchronization of myelinated neural pathways rather than muscular tissue.

## Glial Metabolic Rate
The rate at which oligodendrocytes, astrocytes, and microglia can repair, remodel, and restore neural infrastructure after high‑output work.

## Peroxisomal Throughput
The capacity of peroxisomes to synthesize very‑long‑chain fatty acids (VLCFAs), plasmalogens, and ether‑lipids required for myelin and membrane turnover.

## Myelin Remodeling
Activity‑dependent thickening, repair, and compaction of myelin sheaths that determine conduction velocity and signal fidelity.

## Neural Recovery Window
The 72–120 hour period required for glial cells to restore white‑matter integrity after maximal neural output.

## Conduction Velocity
The speed at which electrical signals travel along myelinated axons, governed by myelin thickness, internode length, and membrane architecture.

## Neural Synchronization
The coordinated firing of motor units that determines force expression, rate coding efficiency, and high‑output stability.

## Rate Coding
The frequency at which motor neurons fire to produce force; limited by myelin quality, neurotransmitter availability, and glial support.

## VLCFA (Very‑Long‑Chain Fatty Acids)
Fatty acids ≥ C22 required for myelin structure, especially nervonic acid (24:1 n‑9).

## Nervonic Acid
A C24:1 monounsaturated VLCFA essential for myelin synthesis and repair; produced via oleic acid elongation.

## ELOVL1 / ELOVL3
Enzymes responsible for elongating oleic acid into eicosenoic, erucic, and ultimately nervonic acid.

## Plasmalogens
Ether‑lipids critical for membrane resilience, antioxidant protection, and myelin stability.

## Astrocytic Reset
The early phase of neural recovery (0–48 hrs) involving neurotransmitter recycling, ion buffering, and metabolic normalization.

## Oligodendrocyte Repair Window
The 24–72 hr period where myelin sheaths undergo structural repair and compaction.

## Microglial Resolution Phase
The 48–120 hr period where microglia clear metabolic debris and restore neural homeostasis.

## Peroxisomal Lipid Processing
The 48–96 hr window where VLCFAs and plasmalogens are synthesized for myelin turnover.
