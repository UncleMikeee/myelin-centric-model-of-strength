This repository contains the core theory and supporting biochemical charts for the Myelin‑Centric Model of Strength — a reframing of strength as a neural phenomenon rather than a muscular one.

## Core Thesis

Natural strength expression is constrained primarily by the nervous system’s ability to remodel and maintain high‑frequency motor pathways — not by muscle tissue.  
Muscle is downstream.  
White matter is upstream.
